Tests the outline of each shape

CONTROLS:
O: toggle outline
Left Mouse Button: increase number of sides of shape
Right Mouse Button: decrease number of sides of shape (min 0)

TESTS:
1) "NONE" CASE
	- EXPECTED:
	
2) "DOWN" CASE
	- EXPECTED: Outline of 5 pixels positioned at the border of shape, flowing inwards
	
3) "UP" CASE
	- EXPECTED: Outline of 5 pixels positioned at the border of shape, flowing inwards
	
4) "AROUND" CASE
	- EXPECTED: Outline of 5 pixels centered on the border of the shape
