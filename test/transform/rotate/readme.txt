test_rotate.exe
Tests rotating an element around a specific point

CONTROLS:
Left Mouse Button: rotate 1 degree clockwise around center point
Right Mouse Button: rotate 1 degree counter-clockwise around center point
R: reset test to initial state
Shift: WHILE HELD reorient instead of rotate
Z: rotate 90 degrees around center point
X: rotate 180 degrees around center point
C: rotate 275 degrees around center point
V: rotate 360 degrees around center point
